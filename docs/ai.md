# AI Integration

## Status
AI is not implemented yet. It is planned after the core trading flow is stable.

## Principles
- Advisory only. AI does not execute trades by itself.
- Explainable. Users can see reasoning and context.
- Optional. Users can enable or disable AI at will.

## Potential Uses
- Suggesting strategy parameters.
- Multi-timeframe market summaries.
- Post-trade analysis and reporting.
